Recipe: Mais Con Yelo



Mais Con Yelo

Ingredients:

1-2 cups shaved ice
1 tbsp sugar or any sweetener that you prefer
1 tbsp corn cereal for toppings (optional)
1/2 cup cream corn (or kernels)
1/2 cup condensed or evaporated milk

Directions

1.) Put creamed corn into a glass
2.) top glass with shaved ice
3.) pour milk over shaved ice
4.) top with corn flakes
5.) Wait for 5 minutes then pour the mixture on the mold
6.) Stir well and add sweetener until desired sweetness is reached
