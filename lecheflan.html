Recipe: Leche Flan



Leche Flan

Ingredients:

10 eggs
1 can condensed milk (14oz)
1 cup fresh milk or evaporated milk
1 cup granulated sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

In a tall glass, layer the first 11 ingredients.
1.) Seperate the yolks from whites, then beat the yolks
2.) Add codensed milk and mix
3.)Pour fresh milk and vanilla
4.)Put mold on top of lit stove, put in the granulated sugar and wait for it to melt
5.) Wait for 5 minutes then pour the mixture on the mold
6.) cover the mold with aluminum foil and let it steam for 30-35 minutes
7.) Let it cool
